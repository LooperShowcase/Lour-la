High School Musical

(HSM)

High School Musical is a popular Disney Channel movie franchise that began in 2006. The franchise centers around the lives of high school students who navigate relationships, school life, and their passion for singing and performing. The original movie follows Troy Bolton (played by Zac Efron), the captain of the basketball team, and Gabriella Montez (played by Vanessa Hudgens), a shy and intelligent student, who both audition for the school musical. Their love for music leads them to break social boundaries and challenge high school stereotypes.

The franchise includes:

  • High School Musical (2006)
  • High School Musical 2 (2007)
  • High School Musical 3: Senior Year (2008)

High School Musical: The Musical: The Series

(HSMTMTS)

A new generation brings back the magic!

High School Musical: The Musical: The Series is a Disney+ mockumentary-style series that follows a new group of students at East High School as they put on a production of the original *High School Musical* movie. The show combines the classic songs of *HSM* with original music, blending new characters with the nostalgia of the original films. It follows the students' journey as they navigate high school life, friendships, and relationships while preparing for the biggest performance of their lives.
